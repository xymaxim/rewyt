# Rewyt --- Rewatch YouTube live streams

Rewyt is a desktop app to rewatch past moments of live streams beyond YouTube's
limits.

Available on Linux, macOS, and Windows. Built on top of
[ypb](https://github.com/xymaxim/ypb) and
[yt-dlp](https://github.com/yt-dlp/yt-dlp/).
